About

Bottle Post Recipes is an open-source web app inspired by the famous bottle post. Our mission is to provide a system that favors high quality recipes regardless of your background. You don't need 100k followers to gain reach on this platform, all you need is a delicious recipe!

How it works üî®

Users at Bottle Post Recipes are allowed to upgrade/downgrade one random recipe per day. This is the most powerful metric our algorithm use for boosting a post. Below is a step-by-step explanation of how it works.

The user reviews the recipe. There are two choices:

üåä Return the recipe to 'the ocean'
This action boosts the recipe's visibility, allowing other users to continue boosting it in turns.

‚ùå Remove the recipe from 'the ocean'
Other users will no longer be able to review this recipe.

At this point, the user will be locked out from this feature for 24 hours to prevent algorithm abuse.

A real world example

On other social platforms, a person can alert 30 of their friends and tell them to engage with their post imediately, thus increasing the chances of boosting its visibility to other users. This won't work at Bottle Post Recipes because all 30 people would need to be randomly picked by server-side calculations, which is very unlikely.
