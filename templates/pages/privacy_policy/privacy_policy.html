Privacy Policy

By accessing and using this application, you agree to be bound by the most up-to-date version of our Privacy Policy. Our policies can be found here.

Information We Collect

We collect user-submitted information such as recipes, comments, and images. Additionally, we use cookies to manage session information securely.

Use of Cookies

Cookies are used in this application to improve security and provide a better user experience. Specifically, we use CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery) tokens to secure user sessions. You can manage cookies in your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect app functionality.

Data Storage

The application integrates with Cloudinary for image storage and uses PostgreSQL databases for storing user data in testing, development, and production environments. Databases are configured to isolate real user data from testing environments to protect user information.

Third-Party Services

We use Cloudinary for image storage and Heroku for deployment. These third-party services may collect additional data as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

Data Deletion

To delete your data, including recipes and comments, please delete your account. Recipes can be deleted individually by opening the edit view.

Upon account creation, users may upload a profile image. Please note that once selected, this profile image cannot be changed. The only way to remove the profile image is by deleting your account, which will permanently delete all associated data, including your profile, recipes, comments, and other contributions.
